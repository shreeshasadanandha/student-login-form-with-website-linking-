BTS

BTS, also known as Bangtan Sonyeondan or Beyond The Scene, is a South Korean boy band that debuted in 2013 under Big Hit Entertainment. The group consists of seven members: RM, Jin, SUGA, j-hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook. They are known for their meaningful lyrics, socially conscious messages, and strong connection with fans known as ARMY. BTS has achieved global recognition, topping charts, winning international awards, and making a powerful impact through music and philanthropy.
